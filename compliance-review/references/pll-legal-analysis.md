### Priority 4a — ECOA / Reg B "Creditor" Classification (added 2026-03-17)

- Under 12 C.F.R. §1002.2(l), the definition of "creditor" includes persons who "regularly refer applicants to creditors, or select or offer to select creditors to whom requests for credit may be made." ClarifiED's recommendation engine selects which lenders a borrower sees. Does this make Desata a "creditor" under Regulation B?
- If yes, what adverse action notice obligations are triggered when a lender is excluded from a borrower's results?
- Does the effects test under ECOA apply to ClarifiED's recommendation algorithm? If so, what testing and documentation obligations follow?
- Does CFPB Circular 2022-03 (algorithmic adverse action) change the analysis for a recommendation engine that does not itself make credit decisions but selects which creditors are presented?
