# Compliance Review Checklist

> **This is an example checklist for a loan recommendation product in higher education.**
> Replace it entirely with your own industry-specific compliance items.
> Each item should be a single verifiable claim that can be rated against a specific regulation.

Review the subject against each item below. For each, cite the specific regulation and rate: ✅ Compliant, ⚠️ Risk, ❌ Non-compliant, or ❓ Insufficient info.

## No-influence requirement
- [ ] Loan recommendations are based on objective, published criteria — not lender relationships, revenue sharing, or institutional preference
- [ ] No lender receives preferential placement or visibility in exchange for compensation
- [ ] Recommendation methodology is publicly available and auditable

## Preferred lender list (PLL) compliance
- [ ] If a PLL is presented, it includes minimum required number of lenders
- [ ] PLL selection criteria are disclosed to borrowers
- [ ] PLL is reviewed and updated on the required schedule
- [ ] No lender is added or removed based on non-disclosed criteria

## Borrower-facing language
- [ ] Language is neutral — no lender is described more favorably than another
- [ ] Terms are accurate: "recommendation" not "endorsement," "borrower" not "customer"
- [ ] Disclosures are present where required (methodology, data sources, limitations)

## Fee disclosure and anti-kickback
- [ ] No revenue sharing, commissions, or referral fees from lenders
- [ ] Fee structure is transparent and disclosed to institutions
- [ ] No indirect compensation that could influence recommendations

## Methodology transparency
- [ ] Recommendation criteria are published and accessible
- [ ] Weighting factors and data sources are disclosed
- [ ] Methodology changes are documented and communicated

## Data handling and privacy
- [ ] User data handling complies with applicable privacy regulations
- [ ] Data sharing with third parties is limited to what the user consents to
- [ ] Data retention and deletion policies are documented

## Marketing claims
- [ ] Claims about the product are accurate and verifiable
- [ ] No claims of regulatory endorsement or approval unless actually granted
- [ ] Comparative claims are substantiated
