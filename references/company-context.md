## Target users
- **Primary audience:** Financial aid officers, directors, and compliance staff at Title IV institutions. Other offices at schools also interested like enrollment, because they want students funded and attending. 
- **What they care about:** Federal compliance, PLL rules, student borrower outcomes, audit readiness
- **What their day looks like:** Processing applications, advising students, reviewing compliance requirements, managing lender relationships. They are experts who know federal student aid inside and out. Not tech people.
- **Secondary audience:** Lenders evaluating ClarifiED as a distribution channel

## Industry and regulatory environment
- **Industry:** Higher education financial aid (Title IV) and private student lending
- **Key regulations:** Higher Education Act (HEA), One Big Beautiful Bill Act (OBBBA) schedule of reductions, preferred lender list (PLL) compliance requirements, Dear Colleague Letters
- **Regulatory bodies:** Department of Education (ED)
- **Compliance requirements:** No-influence requirement for loan recommendations, published methodology, PLL disclosure rules, anti-kickback provisions
- **Regulatory status:** Active — rules still being finalized
